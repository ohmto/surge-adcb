ISO 9001

ISO Certification

ADCP is one of the few property management companies in Abu Dhabi that has been accredited with the ISO certification for Quality Management Systems and Business Continuity. (ISO 9001) and has been recertified for the third consecutive year. ISO 22301 was also acquired in 2015 (Business Continuity).

ISO 22301

ISO Certification

LADCP is one of the few property management companies in Abu Dhabi that has been accredited with the ISO certification for Quality Management Systems and Business Continuity. ISO 22301 was also acquired in 2015 (Business Continuity).

Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors

Alliances & Partnerships

RICS: ADCP is a member of the Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors (RICS) in the MENA region. The RICS is the foremost international real estate professional standards institution with a focus on the strict ethos of standards of member firms’ delivery of their business practices in the real estate industry.

Abu Dhabi Department of Finance

Alliances & Partnerships

DOF: Since 2007, ADCP has managed over 2,000 properties for the Department of Finance (DOF) for the Government of Abu Dhabi. These properties, formerly known as the Khalifa Committee of the Department of Social Services and Commercial Buildings, make up a large portion of the ADCP portfolio. ADCP also manages several properties on behalf of the Crown Prince Court and the Social Care and Minors Affairs Foundation of Abu Dhabi.

AAbu Dhabi Municipality (MoC)

Alliances & Partnerships

ADM: ADCP signed a memorandum of cooperation (MoC) with the Abu Dhabi Municipality (ADM) in 2011. This MoC was in adherence to the newly implemented Tawtheeq system. The system is linked with ADCP’s electronic system, and over 175,000 contracts have been issued to date.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Corporate Social Responsibility

Taking a conscious step toward the betterment of our society, ADCP has driven several initiatives across Abu Dhabi, such as the distribution of fire blankets to all new and renewed contracts. Since 2014, ADCP has undertaken a project to install window locks for all high-risk buildings in Abu Dhabi to alleviate instances of small children falling out of unprotected windows.
