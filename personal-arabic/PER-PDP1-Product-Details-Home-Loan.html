Complimentary Lounge Access

As a Traveller Card member, you enjoy unlimited self access to premium airport lounges across the many cities where you love to travel every year. For more information, please visit adcb.com/lounge

Discounts at Starwood Hotels & Resorts

Book two nights and get the third night free. This is applicable at 175 properties across Europe, the Middle East and Africa, including St. Regis, W Hotels, Westin, Sheraton, Le Meridien and The Luxury Collection. For your complimentary nights, please visit spg.com/mastercard to book your stay.

Complimentary Travel Insurance

Losing your luggage at the airport or falling sick while you are away from home can ruin your travel experience. The Traveller Credit Card comes with insurance to give you the necessary peace of mind to enjoy your trip.

As a Traveller Credit Card holder, you get:

  • Personal accident insurance and medical emergency expenses - up to USD 500,000
  • Daily in-hospital cash benefit – up to USD 100 per day
  • Loss of baggage - up to USD 3,000 with no deductible
  • Delay of baggage - up to USD 300
  • Trip cancellation - up to USD 7,500
  • Trip delays - USD 300
  • Missed connection - USD 300

For more information or claims, visit www.mcpeaceofmind.com

0% Foreign Currency Fees

Just spend AED 5,000 with your credit card every month to enjoy uninterrupted access. Reservations are mandatory and available on a first come, first served basis for up to 14 days in advance. To book tee times, call us on 600 50 2004.

Mobile App & Internet Banking

Manage your credit card account 24/7 with our multi-functional Mobile App and Personal Internet Banking. Pay all your utility bills, mobile phone bills, school & college fee payments, Salik and credit card payments and redeem TouchPoints wherever you are.

  • Mobile Banking App Select which Credit you wish to make payment for and choose “Manage your Credit Cards” tab.
  • Through any of our branches and Cash Deposit Machines
